Report: What Lower Manhattan businesses closed and opened during the pandemic?

Opened Businesses

Of the 127 businesses that opened in Lower Manhattan during the pandemic, 65 are restaurants or bars, 21 are personal or business services, 19 are shops, and 8 are hotels.

Closed Businesses

Of the 218 businesses that closed in Lower Manhattan during the pandemic, 94 are restaurants or bars, 39 are personal or business services, 65 are shops, and 7 are hotels.
